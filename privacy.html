Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-10

NBA Edits Autopilot is a personal automation tool operated by a single individual for their own TikTok account. It is not a public service and does not have other users.

The only data this tool processes is the operator's own TikTok authentication tokens (obtained via TikTok's OAuth flow and stored locally on the operator's own device) and video content the operator supplies for posting.

This tool does not collect, store, or share personal data belonging to any other person. It does not use cookies or third-party trackers, and does not sell or share data with third parties.
